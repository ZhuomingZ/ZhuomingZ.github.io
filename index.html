PhD, UX/Ergonomics

I am an interaction designer and UX researcher, passionate in improving the application of technology in our society through Human-Centered research and design. My research direction focuses on haptics for emotional communication and tangible interaction on smartphone. I develop creative hardware and software experiences that bridging the gap between HCI research and daily interaction.
